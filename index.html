What is HACKX?

HackX 2025, organized by the International School for Skills (ISS), is a national-level hackathon empowering Grade 12 students across Nepal to solve real-world challenges aligned with the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Spanning four months, the event features provincial hackathons and a national finale in Kathmandu, offering mentorship, hands-on learning, and opportunities to develop skills in innovation, teamwork, and technical expertise.
